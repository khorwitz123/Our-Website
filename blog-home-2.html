Prepositions

by Kevin Horwitz

Knowing prepositions is an easy way to enhance grammar skills, and to be able to identify the position of an object. Many people overthink prepositions, and forget exactly what they are/how to identify them so this is a cartoon video that is easy to follow, and touches on the core of what a preposition is. The lesson is created using the website Powtoon to create an animated video lesson about prepositions. The video follows Jeffrey, Jeffrey just got a new job and his boss tells him in order to begin working he needs to take a grammar assessment, which focuses on knowing prepositions.
