The Mission

The Proposal

Here at Technology Empowered we believe business communication and technology skills are important because most IT positions in demand would benefit from their employers being able to communicate with peers, customers, and possibly executives of other companies. So it is important that those technicians know how to properly communicate in a business professional manner. There are plenty IT positions that deals with various ranges of skills therefore it is best to have employers who possess a strong balance of technical proficiency and business communication skills.

Teaching Methods

We use visual learning methods to teach our lessons in hopes of maximizing the retention rate. This allows the student to take notes at their own pace and watch the videos as many times as they need to.

Our Goal

Here at Technology Empowered we offer the best training to put out the top tier of employers seeking job opportunity in the IT field. Our goal is to have over 80% of our students employed by the time they are done training. With the EDCA we believe we can reach more students and increase that percentage.
